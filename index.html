Venezuela

Located in the north of South America, Venezuela is a beautiful paradise. It is home to the most spectacular beaches in the Caribbean, with white sand and crystal-clear waters like 'Los Roques' and 'Morrocoy'. But its magic is not only on the coast; from the tall Tepuy mountains to the snowy peaks of the 'Los Andes', the landscapes are like a dream. It is a land of happy people and amazing nature that everyone loves.
